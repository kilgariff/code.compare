code.compare()

Welcome to code.compare() - Dundee's weekly coding meet-up! Share & compare code with other developers of all backgrounds and skill levels, have a chat, and get some help with your project.

All you need to bring is a laptop with your project set up and a good attitude.

Snacks are provided, and you can pay what you want at the door.

code.compare() is an event run by Dundee MakerSpace. Contact ross@dundeemakerspace.co.uk if you want to come along!
